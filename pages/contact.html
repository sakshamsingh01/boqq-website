Frequently Asked Questions

BOQO bottles are made from 100% biodegradable bagasse-based biopolymers. Unlike traditional plastic that takes 500+ years to decompose, our bottles break down naturally in 120-180 days in soil. They're also FSSAI certified and completely safe for food contact.

Our minimum order quantity varies by product type. For standard bottles, we start from 10,000 units. For custom designs and private labeling, the MOQ is 25,000 units. Contact us for specific requirements and volume discounts.

Our products are certified by FSSAI (Food Safety), BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards), and comply with ISO 17088 standards for biodegradability. We're also working on international certifications for global markets.

BOQO bottles have a shelf life of 12-18 months when stored properly. Once disposed of in appropriate conditions (soil, compost), they biodegrade within 120-180 days, leaving no harmful residues.
