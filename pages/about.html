About BOQO

Building a plastic-free future, one bottle at a time

Our Story

BOQO is a sustainability-driven startup born from a vision to transform agricultural waste into life-changing solutions.

We recognized that sugarcane bagasse - typically considered agricultural waste - could be the key to solving the global plastic crisis. By converting this abundant resource into 100% biodegradable bottles and tableware, we're creating a circular economy that benefits both the environment and rural communities.

Our innovative approach combines cutting-edge biotechnology with sustainable manufacturing to create products that decompose naturally within months, leaving behind no harmful microplastics.
